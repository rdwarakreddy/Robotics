info Program Overview

The Advanced Robotics Engineering course at UniFirst is designed for experienced engineers looking to pivot into high-stakes automation and autonomous systems. This program bridges the gap between theoretical dynamics and practical AI implementation.

Students will engage with state-of-the-art hardware, including collaborative robots (cobots) and ROS2-based mobile platforms. By the end of this curriculum, you will be capable of architecting complex robotic workflows from the ground up.
